Our Story

Middle Yeast began with a love for dough, fermentation, and the flavors that connect cultures.

Growing up around Middle Eastern kitchens meant learning that food is more than a meal — it’s tradition, generosity, and community. At the same time, the craft of pizza inspired us with its simplicity, technique, and universal appeal.

We created Middle Yeast to bring these worlds together.

By combining slow fermentation, fresh ingredients, and time-honored methods, we craft dough that is light, flavorful, and unforgettable. Each pizza and pastry reflects our belief that quality takes patience and that great food starts with great dough.

Our name represents both our roots and our process: Middle for heritage and culture, Yeast for life, growth, and the art of fermentation.

Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite or sharing a meal with family, we want every visit to feel welcoming, comforting, and delicious.

From our kitchen to your table — Oh MY!
