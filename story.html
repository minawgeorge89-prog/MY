🌾 Our Story

Our journey began with a simple love for pizza — and a problem we could no longer ignore.

Every time we enjoyed pizza, someone in our family felt discomfort afterward. Instead of giving it up, we asked a deeper question:

Why does something we love make us feel this way?

That question led us on a journey.

We began studying how dough works — fermentation, hydration, flour types, and the science behind digestibility. We searched for ways to make dough lighter, healthier, and easier on the body.

We explored traditional methods, slow fermentation, and global techniques that transform simple ingredients into something nourishing.

What started as a family concern became a passion.

We experimented. We tested. We learned.

We created our own recipes and refined them over time — discovering that when dough is treated with patience and respect, it not only tastes better… it feels better.

As our journey deepened, we returned to something close to our roots: feteer — a cherished layered pastry from our culture.

We realized that both pizza and feteer share the same heart: dough, time, and care.

So we built our own oven. We crafted our own recipes. We focused on creating food we could trust — food that is fresh, lighter, and made with intention.

Middle Yeast was born from this journey — a place where science meets tradition, and where dough is treated with respect.

A place where food brings comfort instead of discomfort.

A place where family, culture, and flavor come together.

Welcome to where dough meets flavor.
